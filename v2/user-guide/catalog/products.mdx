Products are the core of your e-commerce store. This guide covers everything you need to know about managing your product catalog.

## Overview

The Products section allows you to:

- Create new products
- Manage pricing and inventory
- Organize products with categories and brands
- Configure shipping options
- Optimize for search engines

## Accessing Products

Click on **Products** in the sidebar to access the product management page.

  <img src="/images/v2/products.png" alt="Products List" />
</Frame>

## Creating a Product

To add a new product:

1. Click the **Create** button
2. Fill in the product information
3. Add images and configure options
4. Click **Save**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/v2/product-edit.png" alt="Product Detail" />
</Frame>

### Basic Information

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Name** | Yes | The product name shown to customers |
| **Description** | No | Detailed product description |
| **Brand** | No | Select the product brand |
| **Category** | No | Assign to one or more categories |
| **SKU** | No | Stock Keeping Unit for inventory |

### Pricing

Configure your product's pricing:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/v2/product-pricing.png" alt="Product Pricing" />
</Frame>

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Price** | The regular selling price |
| **Compare at price** | Original price (for showing discounts) |
| **Cost per item** | Your cost (used for profit calculations) |

### Inventory

Manage stock levels for your product:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/v2/product-inventory.png" alt="Product Inventory" />
</Frame>

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Track inventory** | Enable stock tracking |
| **Quantity** | Current stock level |
| **Continue selling when out of stock** | Allow backorders |

You can add new inventory:

### Shipping

Configure shipping options:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/v2/product-shipping.png" alt="Product Shipping" />
</Frame>

|-------|-------------|
| **Physical product** | Requires shipping |

Optimize your product for search engines:

<Frame>
